Example Usage

After creating a volume, you can:

  1. Upload individual files or ZIP archives to the volume
  2. Mount the volume to containers during deployment
  3. Access the files from within your containerized applications

Common use cases: Configuration files, database data, uploaded assets, shared libraries, certificates
